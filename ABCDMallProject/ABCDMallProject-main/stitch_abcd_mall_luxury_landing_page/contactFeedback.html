Frequently Asked Questions

search
What are the mall operating hours? expand_more
ABCD Mall is open Monday through Friday from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. On weekends and public holidays, we extend our hours from 10:00 AM to 11:30 PM to ensure you have plenty of time for luxury shopping and dining.
Is parking available for visitors? expand_more
Yes, we offer premium multi-level parking for over 2,000 vehicles. We also provide professional valet services at the main entrance and electric vehicle charging stations on level P2.
How do I become a Mall Elite member? expand_more
Membership to ABCD Mall Elite is complimentary. You can sign up at our concierge desk or through our mobile app. Members enjoy exclusive benefits like free valet parking, lounge access, and early notifications for luxury brand sales.
Are pets allowed inside the mall? expand_more
Small pets in carriers or strollers are allowed in the common areas. However, individual store policies may vary, and pets are generally not permitted in food courts or dining areas for hygiene reasons.
